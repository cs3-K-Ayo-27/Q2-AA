Engr. Ramil A. Sanchez

Engr. Ramil A. Sanchez is the Campus
Director of the Philippine Science High
School - Caraga Region Campus. He is
also known as the “Father of Pisay
Caraga”.





Pisay Caraga provides scholars with an exceptional learning environment through its
numerous facilities and buildings. The school's library, equipped with Wi-Fi and
computers, supports students in meeting their academic requirements, allowing them
to borrow books and access the library's Wi-Fi. Additionally, Pisay offers recreational
spaces such as a STEM park and a grand area for post-school relaxation and various
sports activities like frisbee and volleyball on the field.


To accommodate students from distant locations, Pisay Caraga offers two dormitories:
Dormitory 1 and Dormitory 2. The school is well-equipped with specialized areas,
including a Fab Lab, Adtech Room, Chemistry Lab, Physics Lab, Biology Lab, and three
computer labs. With four academic buildings, each featuring three floors, as well as a
gymnasium and a Student Activity Center on the third floor of the canteen building,
Pisay Caraga ensures a comprehensive learning environment. Additionally, the school
boasts a two-floor canteen. Currently, an under-construction building is in progress at
Pisay Caraga.





Pisay Caraga, distinguished for its exceptional achievements in local, regional, and
international competitions, stands as a beacon of academic excellence. The school
proudly showcases a remarkable track record, having produced 331 graduates from
the Classes of 2020, 2021, 2022, and 2023. A noteworthy aspect of Pisay Caraga's
success lies in the active pursuit of STEM degree programs by its scholars, both within the country and on the global stage.

The scholars of Pisay Caraga are not only academically adept but also excel in various extracurricular activities, earning accolades in areas such as debating and publication. Perhaps most commendably, these scholars consistently demonstrate their commitment to national service, contributing significantly to the betterment
of the community and the nation as a whole. Embodying the spirit of academic prowess and civic responsibility, Pisay Caraga scholars adhere to the institution's
guiding principle encapsulated in the quote, "Study well, Behave well." This motto reflects the school's commitment not only to intellectual development but also to
the cultivation of good character among its students.

In essence, Pisay Caraga stands as a testament to the harmonious blend of academic excellence, extracurricular achievements, and a sense of social responsibility, shaping well-rounded individuals poised to make meaningful contributions to society.
